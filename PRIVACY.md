# Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 20, 2026

Rune does not collect personal data or any other user data.

We do not use analytics, tracking, advertising, or telemetry to collect information about you, your usage, or your environments.

We do not collect, transfer, or sell your data. We will not do so in the future.

The only data transmitted over a network is the requests and responses required for Rune to communicate with the Kubernetes clusters and services that you choose to connect to.

Rune does not send your data to our servers, because we do not operate any backend for collecting, analyzing, or selling user data.

Any information handled by the app is used locally on your device to display resources, logs, configuration, and other responses from your clusters. If the app stores local settings, cache, or connection-related information, that data remains on your device and is not used for our data collection.

If you choose to export files or save diagnostics, that happens only at your direction.

If this policy ever changes, the change will be clearly communicated in the app or wherever Rune is distributed before it takes effect.
